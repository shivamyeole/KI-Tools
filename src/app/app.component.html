Atos Global B&PS KI is responsible for effective implementation of KI tools and processes across Atos globally. The key to effective implementation of tools is increase in number of tools usage by providing efficient support. KI supports all Atos countries for administration and maintenance of SDLC tools to achieve standardized, globalized, centralized organization of tools within Atos. Management of domains and projects is centrally organized by the KI.
