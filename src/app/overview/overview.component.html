WELCOME TO KI TOOLS

SSC (Shared Service Center) is now KI (Knowledge Intelligence) !!      Dear ATOS Global B&PS KI Users, we kindly remind you that all Support Tickets and Requests must be addressed in English Language.

Atos Global B&PS KI is responsible for effective implementation of KI tools and processes across Atos globally. The key to effective implementation of tools is increase in number of tools usage by providing efficient support. KI supports all Atos countries for administration and maintenance of SDLC tools to achieve standardized, globalized, centralized organization of tools within Atos. Management of domains and projects is centrally organized by the KI.

KI Tools Landscape:

KI Tools Team mainly deals with Application Support & Maintenance activities comply with contract guidelines.

Application Support is following inbound Service Level Agreements whose base foundation is Quality, Resolution Time, and Communication with End users.

Application Maintenance mainly focuses on mitigation of KI in-house developed products based on customer Inputs.

Our B&PS KI-Tools on going Journey

Global B&PS has invested since twelve years on software from main vendors (IBM, GitHub, Microfocus/HPE, CAST, SNOW) to select best of breed solutions for our Atos B&PS Projects.

Our multiple ELA (Enterprise License Agreements) help us in anticipating any usage growth with our software suppliers. Furthermore, advanced support with our vendors guarantee high level of SLA to ensure continuous services delivery for our B&PS contracts deployed on our platform.

Dedicated Atos B&PS KI teams design, release, deploy corporate processes and technical templates which are bringing our DNA inside our way of working implementing industrialization, automation in all our SLDC phases. We also follow regular software upgrade to take benefits of latest functional features and performance improvements which offer real advantages to commit reliability and sustainability of our investments. This is a continuous journey as we keep on introducing new solutions in line with IT trend as we want to keep opened all capabilities for DevSecOps, TestOps as keystones of our B&PS Digital transformation. GitHub is used as central pillar for BPS FR DevOps Foundry From 2020 Q3 onwards, our Infra will move to new Cloud Platform where seamless integration with DevOps Foundry will be easily manageable. BPS KI-SSC GitHub will be our central HUB for Inner Sourcing.

Working jointly with our Atos IDM Team since 2010, we deployed such software on our premises ensuring high level of security, reliability and scalability regarding our hardware capabilities.
